About Us

AGRICULTURE REVOLUTION

In today's world, agriculture faces numerous challenges, from unpredictable weather patterns to crop diseases and soil degradation. Our project, BhoomiAI, aims to tackle these agricultural difficulties using technology, bringing innovative solutions to small-scale farmers who often struggle with limited resources. Our project aims to support small-scale farmers by predicting crop diseases, analyzing soil quality, providing weather forecasts, and offering a personalized verbal chatbot assistant.
